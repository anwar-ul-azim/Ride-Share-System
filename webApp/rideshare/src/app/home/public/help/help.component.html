Creating a CHOLO Account

To get a CHOLO ride, you need to create a CHOLO account with an email ID. With this, you can log in to CHOLO.

Registering as a Passenger

After creating your CHOLO account, you can register as a passenger.
This can be done by updating your PROFILE after you have Logged In. You need to select the option of "Passenger" under "Type".

Registering as a Driver

After creating your CHOLO account, you can register as a driver.
This can be done by updating your PROFILE after you have Logged In. You need to select the option of "Driver" under "Type".
You now also neeed to select the vehicle that you will use under the tab "Vehicle".

Get a CHOLO Ride

To get a CHOLO ride you need to select your Starting Point and Destination from "Get Direction". Then you will see CHOLO Vehicle that are situated nearby on the Map. Now you can choose a Vehicle. On hovering over a vehicle, you will see the driver's contact number and name. You can then contact with the driver and get a CHOLO ride.
